CHAPTER I.

Hello, world!

TMF was beginning to get very tired of napping every time, and of having nothing to do: once or twice she had peeped into the possibility of youtubing, but she always slept it off, "and what is the use of your life," thought TMF, "without videos or writings!"

So she was considering in her own mind, whether the pleasure of making a bad youtube video would be worth the trouble, when suddenly a white rabbit with pink eyes ran close by her.
